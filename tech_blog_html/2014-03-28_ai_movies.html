Artificial Intelligence on Movies and books primarily give us insights into the details of human imagination on artificial intelligence. Many of these movies are based on science fiction books. While recreating and retelling the stories of artificial intelligence some movies have been successful in drawing viewer’s interest bringing artificial intelligence and relevant fields such as cognitive science, data mining, representation, logics, bioinformatics, human computer interaction and design of user of interface together. We see examples of following those imaginary stories and bringing dreams into reality in modern times with innovation of technology and science. While some movies present problems with human emotion and machine interaction to make it further interesting some of those actually provide some details into implementation of A.I ideas. Though far from reality, sometimes they are so high quality fascination that are likely to take you into a journey of different world. 2001: A Space Odyssey (1968)

This movie is ranked as 106th on imdb chart. However, the central story is complicated. Without knowledge on the subject of the story some people found the movie slow and boring. However, this movie draws a good theme on story of evolution of intelligent beings. And, this evolution is very much related to vicinity of a monolith. Artificially intelligent HAL computers become critically obstacle in achieving human evolution what is required to evolve by travelling to moon and reaching the monolith that is placed there. In a space journey for that, first ever conflict between evolution of human beings and artificially intelligent robotic systems erupts. It is to wonder who finally wins! This movie is highly reputed for extra-ordinary making and creation. Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope (1977)

Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope (1977) is about winning a war of rebellion against evil force. The rebellion is led by Princess Liea and, evil force is led by Lord Dark Vader. Some robotic devices with limited artificial intelligence capability have been presented in the movie. One of the notable robot characters is round-shaped, wheeled robot named R2 Unit who commotes with another human shaped Robot.

The droid R2 Unit is used to store some secret information by the princess. Though authentication procedure is not presented it however recognizes authentic recipient for the information and presents intended messages. With master Obi-Wan Kenobi it played a major role in winning the battle. This movie also gives us a view of a world where many strange species are displayed in a place named Alderaan. A.I: Artificial Intelligence (2001)

In this movie, A.I an artificially intelligent robot is manufactured by a company which looks exactly like the son a couple recently lost. However intelligent or looking alike in appearance it is difficult to replace a human child.

When the notorious real child is found back it takes a twist in the story. Later, time comes to give this robot back to the manufacturer company for disposal. With uncontrolled emotion of love for this human-looking robot, the mother cannot leave the robot for disposal. She leaves the robot boy in a forest. Being free now, though missing his unreal mom, it creates further adventurous stories. But the boy only dreams to get back to her human mother for who his love never recesses.
