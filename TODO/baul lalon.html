If we analyze Bauls we find Baul means those who through religious way communicate with air to perform worship and self-meditation. As the baul says “Catch the air first, by learning its ways, catch the air, aim the target and sit waiting, O my heart” In 18th-19th century rural Bengal society tried to change. The reason was Brahmanism and Islamic fundamentalism became so severe that common people felt shelter-less. As a result during 18th-19th centuries quite a few minor religious sects emerged in Bengal by the initiative and leadership of common people. One characteristic of these sects was there was generally no difference between Hindus and Muslims. It was quite common to find a Muslim Guru with Hindu disciples or a Hindu Guru with Muslim disciples. One main reason was the influence of Sree Chaitanya. Vaisnavism simplified the scriptures and other difficult formalities of religion. From Vaisnavism common people learnt that to worship God one do not need much of fasting, vow, hymns, scriptures, Sanskrit or Arabic languages. One can worship God straightway only an intermediary known as Guru or Murshid is needed. One of Lalon’s song says,
“আমারে কি রাখবেন গুরু চরণদাসী? ইতরপনা কার্য আমার অহর্নিশি।। জঠর যন্ত্রণা পেয়ে এলাম যে করার দিয়ে রইলাম তা সব ভুলিয়ে ভবে আসি।। চিনলাম না সে গুরু কি ধন জানলাম না তার সেবা সাধন ঘুরতে বুঝি হল রে মন চোরাশি।। গুরু যার থাকে সদায় শমন বলে তার কিসের ভয় লালন বলে, মন তুই আমার করলি দুষি।।“ “Will Guru keep me as disciple? I succumb to base things Day and night Will Guru keep me as disciple During birth-pang I made promise I have forgotten the pledge After coming out on to this world Will Guru keep me as disciple? To whom Guru is kind He is not afraid of death’s summon Fakir Lalon says O my heart You make me feel guilty Will Guru keep me as disciple? I often succumb to base things Day and night Will Guru keep me as disciple?”
In our Bengali culture we find that the Buddhist simplistic doctrine which was there since the beginning of Bengali literature has some reflection on the philosophy of the bauls. Then we notice the Vaisnava way of meditation also seeped in baul ideas. Later on, when the Sufi’s came here they also brought along with a form of meditation. We observe a synthesis of all of these in Lalon’s philosophy. Their way of worship was esoteric. They can’t divulge it to everybody. Their worship is body centred. That’s why for their safety they didn’t want to reveal it to public. Surroundings were against them. Common villagers acknowledged that they were sect with a different kind of ideas and philosophy and called them Nara’s Fakir. People enjoyed their songs, but weren’t keen to mix with them. But, Lalon was not a traditional baul. He had distinct differences with other sects. In those sects i.e. “Sahebdhani” “kartabhaza” “balrambhaza” we find they all have patron-master ways, own hermitages. The master gradually turns himself into a high priest and the disciples start to pay offerings and penalties. Thus those religions gradually become perverted. Drug addiction is their synonym of devotion! Lalon said,
“প্রেম জানো না প্রেমের হাতের ফুল দোলা ও তার কথায় দেখি দম্ভওয়ালা মনে কল-ষোলকলা প্রেম জানো না প্রেমের হাতের ফুল দোলা বেশ করে সে বোষ্টমগিরী রস নাহি ধুম্রভারি হরি নামে ধুঁধুঁ টানে তিন গাছে তার গাঁথে মালা।“ [এই গানটি পান্ডুলিপির সাথে যাচাই করা প্রয়োজন, থাকলে মন্তব্যে প্রকাশ করুন] “You don’t know love Only babble in love-market You talk holy But your heart is crooked You don’t know love Only babble in love-market Dressed as a Krishna-lover he pretends But has no real taste of love You pray with rosary But without any devotion You don’t know love Only babble in love-market All exorcise are pretensions Goblins are bogey You just roam In love market Without any taste of love You don’t know love Only babble in love-market”
Lalon was completely different. Lalon didn’t want to start a system like that. Instead of master-disciple Lalon expressed his philosophy through songs only. We should bear in mind that Lalon Fakir was a humanist and lyricist, a creative artist. That’s why the most important vehicle for him was not mantra-scripture, not even hermitage. His most important medium was his songs. Essence of Lalon songs that is to search the soul inside human beings,
“I am in this room but I’m not present here” “Or only by calling inside I will find the man ” “You are hiding in my heart and I didn’t see you” “সত্য বল, সুপথে চল ওরে আমার মন সত্য সুপথ না চিনিলে পাবিনে মানুষের দরশন পরের দ্রব্য পরের নারী হরণ করো না পারে যেতে পারবানা যতবার করিবে হরণ ততবারই হবে জনম” “Tell truth, thread righteous path O my uninitiated mind”
